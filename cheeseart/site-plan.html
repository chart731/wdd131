Cheese Does Art Site Plan

Emma Bateman

WDD 131-A2

Overview

Purpose

To showcase different works of art from a family member, and give them the opportunity to share how they got where they are at today, and an opportunity to share their art with many different people.

Audience

General art community, teenagers and young adults that are looking for commissioned works of art.

Dynamic elements

I am going to use the parent page as a gallery/information section, so I am going to use a modal/viewer window for the pictures on that page as a dynamic element. For the second page, it will be more of a shop/selection page, where users can look at the artworks from the previous page and choose which ones they would like to buy. There will be working buttons and inputs that will allow the user to choose how many they would like to buy of each item, and it will then add up the total amount that the user owes.
